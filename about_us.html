About Us

Bringing Joy, One Toy at a Time

Welcome to Shilpra Play House , where imagination comes to life! We're more than just a toy store — we're a place where childhood dreams take shape, giggles are shared, and every toy tells a story.

Who We Are

We’re a team of passionate parents, educators, and dreamers on a mission to create and curate toys that spark creativity, build skills, and bring smiles to little faces. With a deep understanding of child development, we believe that play is the most powerful way for children to learn and grow.

What We Offer

From educational puzzles and wooden classics to cuddly soft toys and innovative STEM kits, every product we offer is carefully selected for its quality, safety, and ability to inspire.
  • 100% child-safe materials
  • Fun + learning in every toy
  • Age-appropriate collections for every stage
  • Loved by kids, trusted by parents

Why Choose Us?

We care about what goes into your child’s hands. Our toys are handpicked not just to entertain, but to nurture essential life skills — from problem-solving and communication to creativity and confidence. With every toy we deliver, we hope to add a little more wonder to your child’s world.

Our Promise

We’re committed to quality, safety, and smiles. Whether you're shopping for a birthday, a learning milestone, or just a reason to play — we're here to help you make it magical.

Shipra is your one-stop destination for high-quality products across categories like toys, home essentials, educational tools, and more. We are committed to offering a seamless shopping experience and affordability.
