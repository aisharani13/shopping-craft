Shipping & Delivery Policy

Welcome to Shipra Play House – where creativity meets childhood joy! We are committed to delivering safe, high-quality toys right to your doorstep with care and efficiency.

1. Shipping Locations

We currently ship across India. If your delivery address is outside our standard coverage area or in a remote location, our team will contact you to confirm feasibility and timelines.

2. Order Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

3. Estimated Delivery Time

  • Metro Cities: 3–5 business days
  • Other Locations: 5–7 business days
  • Remote/PIN code-sensitive areas: May take 7–10 business days
Note: Delays may occur due to weather, festivals, or unforeseen courier issues.

4. Shipping Charges

  • Free Shipping on all orders above ₹[Insert threshold, e.g., ₹999].
  • • Orders below the threshold may include a nominal shipping charge, visible at checkout.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:
  • A shipping confirmation email or SMS
  • A tracking ID and courier details
  • A link to monitor real-time delivery status
You can also log in to your Shipra Play House account to view your order status.

6. Packaging & Safety

We pack all toys with care using eco-friendly, child-safe, and tamper-proof materials to ensure that your product reaches you in perfect condition

7. Delivery Partners

We partner with trusted logistics services like Delhivery, Bluedart, DTDC, and India Post to provide timely and safe delivery across the country.

8. Missed or Delayed Deliveries

  • If you’re not available at the time of delivery, the courier will attempt to deliver again.
  • In case of repeated failed attempts, the package may be returned to us.
  • If you experience delays, please contact us with your order number for quick assistance.

9. International Shipping

At this time, Shipra Play House does not offer international shipping.
We currently cater only to customers within India.
